Break Free helps you build better habits with a calm, gamified experience—so progress feels motivating, not punishing.

WHAT YOU GET

• Habit tracking — Add habits, check in on clean days, and grow day streaks.
• Streak protection — Weekly Freeze and a Streak Shield (from Level 3) so you can recover without losing everything after one slip.
• Levels & rewards — Earn XP and coins, unlock titles from Beginner to Hero of Break Free.
• Milestones — Celebrate 3, 7, 14, 30+ day streaks with clear next goals.
• Relapse support — Log triggers when you slip; optional confirmation before resetting a streak.
• Panic button — Fast access when you need grounding in the moment.
• Mind City — Watch your city grow as you level up.
• Journal — Space for reflection.

Break Free is designed for self-improvement and mindfulness. It is not a substitute for professional medical or mental health care.
